Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Luvo and our travel services

General Questions

Luvo is a comprehensive travel planning platform that helps you explore destinations, compare travel options, and plan itineraries all in one place. Whether you're a student looking for affordable trips, a family planning a vacation, or a local business wanting to connect with travelers, Luvo provides the tools you need.

Yes! Luvo is free for travelers to search, explore, and plan trips. We offer premium features for enhanced planning capabilities, and businesses pay a small commission on bookings made through our platform.

Simply create an account, search for your desired destination, and browse through accommodations, activities, and local experiences. You can compare prices, read reviews, and book everything directly through Luvo. Our Family Planner tool also helps you collaborate with travel companions and manage budgets together.

Luvo covers destinations worldwide, from major cities to hidden gems. We're constantly adding new locations and local businesses to our platform. If you don't find a specific destination, you can request it, and we'll work on adding it.

Booking & Payment

To make a booking, search for your destination, select the service you want (accommodation, activity, etc.), choose your dates, and click "Book Now." You'll be guided through a secure checkout process where you can review your booking and make payment.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, and various digital payment methods depending on your region. All transactions are encrypted and secure.

Cancellation and modification policies vary by provider. Most bookings can be cancelled or modified up to 24-48 hours before your scheduled date. You can view specific policies on each listing's page and manage your bookings from your account dashboard.

Yes! You'll receive an instant email confirmation with all your booking details, including confirmation numbers, addresses, and contact information. You can also access all your bookings anytime from your Luvo account.

No hidden fees! The price you see during checkout is the final price you pay. This includes all applicable taxes and service fees. Some accommodations or activities may have optional add-ons, but these are clearly marked.

Account & Profile

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, fill in your basic information (name, email, password), and verify your email address. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for faster registration.

Click on "Login" and then select "Forgot Password." Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

Log into your account, click on your profile icon, and select "Account Settings." From there, you can update your personal information, contact details, payment methods, and preferences.

Yes, you can delete your account anytime from Account Settings. Please note that deleting your account will remove all your saved trips, preferences, and booking history. Active bookings must be cancelled before account deletion.

For Businesses

Visit our Business Portal and click "List Your Business." Fill out the registration form with your business details, upload photos, and set your pricing and availability. Our team will review your submission within 2-3 business days.

Luvo charges a commission on completed bookings, typically between 10-15% depending on your service type and booking volume. There are no upfront listing fees or monthly subscriptions. You only pay when you receive bookings.

Access your Business Dashboard to view all bookings, update availability, manage pricing, and communicate with customers. You'll receive real-time notifications for new bookings and inquiries.

Payments are processed and transferred to your account within 24-48 hours after the service is completed. You can track all your earnings and transactions in your Business Dashboard.
